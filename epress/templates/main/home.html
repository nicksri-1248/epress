Our Process

This is how we Work

Schedule Your Collection

The website will allow customers to schedule laundry pick-ups at a time that is convenient for them. Once the hotel has made a booking, our drivers will arrive at the hotel to collect the laundry.

Pack Your Laundry

The website will provide instructions for packing laundry,including tips on how to separate different types of laundry and how to label items to avoid any mix-ups.

Wait for Our Driver

Once the laundry has been packed and ready for collection, the customer can sit back and relax while our driver picks up the laundry. Our drivers will arrive on time and collect the laundry from the hotel reception.

Relax While We Take Care of Your Laundry

Once the laundry has been collected, our laundry staff will take care of the rest. We will wash, dry, fold and deliver the laundry back to the hotel at a time that is convenient for them.
